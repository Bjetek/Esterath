Story

Somewhere in the World of Esterath, in the land of the Holy Dragons, Alornard is once again targeted by the merciless Reughtar. As a result of the brutal conflict, the main commander of the elite group of hunters, Drakhan, is killed. Alornard falls, and only seven steadfast survivors emerge from the battlefield in main camp unscathed: Emeran - Drakhan's son, Zultan - Drakhan's fearless companion, Worgen - a master blacksmith of unparalleled skill, Iris - a healer with a heart full of compassion, Kahyn - Emeran's loyal friend, Ajna - the savvy daughter of healer Iris, and Nidia - the brave daughter of blacksmith Worgen. After the fall of Alornard, the survivors are forced to flee to Clenyia. However, the bloodthirsty Reughtar wants to complete his destruction and begins hunting the refugees, sowing fear and uncertainty in their hearts. From now on, everyone must rely only on themselves, surviving in loneliness and pain that cannot be soothed. Despite this, Emeran does not intend to give up - he decides to stand at the head of the Dragon Team, uniting the survivors. Brought together by their desire to regain their lost legacy, they devise a desperate plan. With undying hope as the only beacon in the darkness of their path, they embark on a risky expedition in search of the Holy Dragons....

World of
Esterath

The world of Esterath is a land where weakness has no place. Where once human drew lines between good and evil, wild moss now grows, and dense bushes cover forgotten traces. The boundaries you set should be exactly where you fell you need them. Every choice you make could determine the fate of you and your companions, so let your decisions be wise, and your boundaries–well thought out. Stand by the side of loyal companions, fight for freedom, uncover forgotten secrets, and explore vibrant places that hide untold histories. Weave yourself into this beautiful, dark, and unpredictable tale, and experience an adventure that will stay with you forever.
