Dragon's Paradise is a mysterious and mystical land of Sacred Dragons, hidden deep within the reach of human comprehension, where majestic dragons rest in sacred sleep, surrounded by the protection of loyal guards. This amazing land, filled with an aura of divine serenity, is accessible only through a single portal of heaven, which is located at the top of the Misty Mountain in Clenyia. This portal is guarded by the legendary Dragon Guardian, who constantly watches over the entrance to this sacred place. Legend has it that anyone who dares to go through the harsh trials, overcomes all obstacles and awakens the sleeping dragons will gain their lifelong help and unwavering protection. The Dragon's Paradise, full of mystical powers and undiscovered secrets, offers not only refuge, but also a blessing that can change the fate of anyone who proves his worth in the eyes of these powerful guardians.
